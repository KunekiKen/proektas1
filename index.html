About Site

This site tells foreigners about where you can go to Lithuania at any time of the year. Here you will find recommendations for the best places for relaxation and attractions suitable for each season - whether it be summer beaches and picturesque lakes, autumn parks and festivals, winter fairs and rinks, or spring gardens and historical monuments. We have collected the most interesting routes and tips so that your journey through Lithuania becomes bright, comfortable and memorable!

Curonian braid

In the summer, in Lithuania, the Curonian Spit becomes obligatory to visit the place. This unique natural reserve, located on the border of Lithuania and the Kaliningrad region, is known for its picturesque dunes and pine forests. Walking on soft sand, cycling along the coast and observing rare species of birds will be an unforgettable summer adventure. Be sure to look into the town of Nida, which is famous for cozy cafes, museum expositions and atmosphere of calm.

Palanga beaches

Palanga is the main resort city of Lithuania on the shores of the Baltic Sea. In summer, here you can enjoy clean beaches, warm sun and various entertainments. In addition to relaxing on the beach, you can take a walk along the famous pedestrian street Basanavichaus, visit the Palance Botanical Garden and the Tishuskassky Palace, where the amber museum is located. In the evening, look at the pier and admire the sunset over the sea.

Trukai and Lake Galva

On hot summer days, go to Truka to enjoy the beauty of nature and historical attractions. The Castle Castle, built on the island in the middle of Lake Galva, is one of the most popular tourist attractions of Lithuania. Here you can rent a boat or kayak, ride water bicycles and discover unique views of the castle and surrounding forests. This picturesque corner is great for a summer picnic and a relaxed vacation in nature.

Druskininkai

In the fall, the resort city of Druskininkai becomes especially beautiful. Golden and crimson shades of leaves are reflected in numerous ponds and rivers, creating a magical atmosphere. Relax in one of the famous spa centers of the city, go through cozy parks and enjoy the calmness of the autumn forest. In Druskininkai, you can also visit a water park and wellness procedures.

National Park Auksteutia

This national park is especially beautiful in the fall, when the trees are covered with gold and a crimson. Here you can go on foot and bicycle routes, enjoy autumn landscapes, walk along the forest paths and open clean lakes for yourself. Auksteutia offers cozy corners for picnics, where you can relax in the fresh air and admire picturesque landscapes.

Harvesting Feast in Kaunas

In September, in Kaunas, a traditional harvest fee takes place, where local farmers represent their products, and you can try fresh vegetables, fruits and delicious cheeses. The holiday takes place on the central streets of the city and creates a special autumn mood. Visit Kaunas in the fall to get acquainted with local traditions, taste seasonal dishes and enjoy the rich taste of Lithuanian autumn.

Vilnius for Christmas

In winter, a real Christmas fairy tale reigns in Vilnius. In the old city, thousands of lights are lit, and the Christmas tree on the Cathedral Square fascinates with its beauty. Take a walk along the cozy streets, visit holiday fairs, enjoy hot mulled wine and local varnishes. Vilnius in winter is an atmosphere of comfort and a holiday that will create a mood even on the most frosty days.

The ski resort Laisvelas

In Lithuania there is a small ski resort Laisvelas, which is suitable for novice skiers and lovers of winter sports. The resort is equipped with skiing and snowboarding, as well as tubing slopes for children and adults. After outdoor activities, you can warm up in cozy cafes with hot chocolate or have lunch in local restaurants offering traditional cuisine.

Grotto and ice caves in a palang

In the winter season, the Baltic coast near the palange turns into a real ice gallery. Local grottoes and rocks are covered with ice, forming fascinating forms. Ice caves are an unusual place that is worth seeing in winter. Take beautiful photos against the backdrop of ice sculptures, enjoy fresh sea air and silence of the winter coast.

Vilnius gardens

In spring, the old town of Vilnius and its gardens bloom, turning into an amazing sight. A walk through the Bernardine Garden or the Vingis Park will fill you with bright colors and scents of flowers. In April-May, Vilnius Gardens become a popular place for citizens and tourists to relax, where you can admire spring flowers, sit by the ponds and just enjoy nature.

The Hill of Crosses is one of the most amazing and iconic places in Lithuania, and in spring, when nature comes to life, it is especially beautiful here. This unique place with thousands of crosses of different sizes and shapes has a deep cultural and spiritual meaning for Lithuanians. Visit it in spring, when the peace and quiet of this place is especially felt, and feel the grandeur and history of Lithuania.

The spring air here is filled with sea freshness and warmth, and the atmosphere of the city creates a pleasant mood for leisurely walks and acquaintance with its sights.
